Brand Guide

"Cool Treats for Cool Pups!"

Concept

Yo Doggies is a frozen yogurt shop made especially for dogs. Our mission is to provide healthy, tasty, and fun frozen treats so every pup can enjoy dessert time with their humans.

Colors

  • Primary Color: #ff6f61 (Playful Coral)
  • Secondary Color: #6ec6ff (Sky Blue)
  • Accent Color: #ffd166 (Sunny Yellow)
  • Neutral Background: #fdfdfd (Soft White)

Fonts

  • Headings: Comic Sans MS, cursive (Fun, Playful)
  • Body: Arial, sans-serif (Clean, Easy to Read)
